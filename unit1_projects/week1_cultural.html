DBC Culture

The DBC culture is intense. It places great expectations on its participants for integrity and persistance. At DBC, particpants get as much out of the program as they put in. I think that this is generally true of many endeavors, particularly those with a learning focus. I think that this is exciting.

DBC is also highly collaborative, despite the emphasis on self-driven learning. This is a culture reflective of a real working environment.
